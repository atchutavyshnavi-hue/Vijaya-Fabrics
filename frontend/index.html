Our Story

Vijaya Fabrics, Chirala

Chirala is one of Andhra Pradesh's oldest handloom towns, and we've stayed close to that craft — sourcing directly from weaving families in Chirala, Mangalagiri and Pochampalli, alongside silk from Surat and pattu from Banaras and Dharmavaram.

Why Customers Come Back

We know the fabric, not just the price tag

Every saree in our catalog is tagged by its fabric and weaving origin, so you know exactly what you're buying — whether it's a light Sico pattu for a day event or a heavy Dharmavaram pattu for a wedding.

Visit our shop in Chirala to feel the fabric yourself, or message us on WhatsApp with a saree name for same-day photos and pricing.
